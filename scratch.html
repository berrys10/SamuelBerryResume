Top Tips for Wakeboarding

  1. Preparation:
    • Choose the right gear
    • Practice on land first
    • Ask a friend to borrow their boat
  2. On the Water:
    • Start with a beginner stance, like your sitting in a chair
    • Keep your knees bent
    • Yell "hit it" or "go" when you are ready to get going.
  3. Safety:
    • Always wear a life jacket
    • Know your limits
    • Don't be afraid to try something new
